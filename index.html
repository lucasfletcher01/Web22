Web22

Refreshingly Ambitious


The Web 2022 Showcase is an event where you can speak to our students in the following sectors: Web Design, Web Technology, Web Programming and Web Programming with Cyber Security students. We will be showcasing the skills we have gained on the course with the support of our work. Our students are delighted to show off their work and themselves as a person.

Fletcher Films
Lucas Fletcher

Advertisement video produced and edited in Adobe Premier Pro and After Effects. This project uses a combination of stock footage and original assets and demonstrates a scenario where a user utilises the app to book a cinema ticket. The finished video uses various effects involving green screen and the mask tool to insert video inside of a shape layer.
