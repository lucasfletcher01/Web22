REFRESHINGLY
AMBITIOUS

The Web22 Showcase is an event that gives you a chance to meet our Web students and see the fruits of their labour from the last three or four years, whilst at Huddersfield University.

The students are ready to show off their skills and their characters to try and impress you, now they have been released from the shackles of Covid 19. Their motto this year is "refreshingly ambitious", this encapsulates the group's drive and enthusiasm for their work.

Our Work

Cinema App Advert
Lucas Fletcher

Advertisement video produced and edited in Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects. This project uses a combination of stock footage and original assets and demonstrates a scenario where a user utilises the app to book a cinema ticket. The finished video uses various effects involving green screen and the mask tool to insert video inside of a shape layer.

Watch the full video: Fletcher Films Video

Helper App
Will Balmain

This project was undertaken in Year 2 and this was to develop a helper application, for that I created a prototype for a local garden centre. In this I primarily used Adobe XD, Illustrator and Photoshop.

Business Rebrand
Will Balmain

This project is my Advanced Visual Design project in which I created and designed a rebrand for a business in York and for this, I primarily used XD and Illustrator but using more advanced techniques. I had also used Adobe After Effects and Lottie Animations to create interactive animations whilst learning and applying various visual design principles.

STLT Website
Aqeel Zadi

This is called T4 (terminal four) it's a platform in which I built this website for the staff members of the University of Huddersfield, it is about their job descriptions, their experience and their role. Also, this website provides the links to go to the Brightspace website and also to the ipark which provide guidance for the students to learn in the teaching and learning process. It's an online updates website which allows staff to keep updated with new updates every week.

Visit the website: The Strategic Teaching and Learning Team Website

Earfun Website Revamp
Oli Lennox

This project is an overhaul of the Earfun website. This is a company that manufactures audio products. In my opinion their website was quite E-commerce looking and very generic. I have attempted to prototype a new website that is based around the advanced features of the products and how they will benefit the user.

Adobe XD was used to prototype the website. Adobe After Effect was used to make the animations used in the website. I then used the Lottie Files plugin to export these animations into an SVG file that could be run in XD.

Watch a walkthrough of the design: Earfun Prototype Walkthrough

Social Hub Advert
Nathan Wilford

A video made using After Effects that is designed to advertise a product, the product in question for the video being Social Hub, a video and picture sharing platform.

Watch the finished video: Social Hub Video
